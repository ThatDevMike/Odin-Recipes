Jollof Rice Dish

The key to making Ghanian jollof rice (Which we know is the best Jollof Rice} is the stirring of the pot when it is cooking to give its authentic Ghana flavour/p>

One of the things I love, that perhaps needs to obtain when cooking the real jollof rice is shrimp powder that really helps to enhance the overall flavour of the Jollof rice and make it the real deal holyfield!

Ingredients
  • Chopped Tin Red Tomato
  • Tomatoe Puree
  • Tilda Rice
  • Garlic
  • Cloves
  • Oil
  • Shrimp Powder
  • Pepper
  • Three Onions
Steps
  1. Use three onions. Chop one onion in half,use one half for the oil and fry together and put two black cloves to flavour the oil.
  2. Once the oil is fried add one whole tube of tomatoe puree into the oil
  3. Now blend two and a half onions, two garlic cloves, one and a half habenero pepper and two cans of chhopped tomatoes and one black clove and blend together
  4. Now add the blended tomatoes mixture into the oil, add salt, add a pinch of shrimp powder and then boil the stew till it is cooked and then add 5 cups of tilda rice and leave it took cook, whilst stirring it occassionally
